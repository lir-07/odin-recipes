Hamburger Recipe

Description

Hamburger is a classic American dish that consists of a ground beef patty served in a bun, often with various toppings. It's a popular choice for casual meals and barbecues.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into patties, making them slightly larger than the buns.
  3. Season both sides of the patties with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired doneness.
  5. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patties on the bottom buns.
  8. Add your favorite toppings and condiments.
  9. Top with the other half of the bun and serve.
