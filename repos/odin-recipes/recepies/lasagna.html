Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add marinara sauce to the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers.
  7. Finish with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
